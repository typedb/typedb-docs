= TypeDB Academy
:page-no-toc: 1

[#_blank_heading]
== {blank}


TypeDB Academy is an end-to-end learning experience for TypeDB and TypeQL. Through the lens of a sample bookstore database, you'll learn how to read data, write data, and build schemas while taking advantage of TypeDB's unique features as a modern, type-safe, and highly expressive database.

This course is intended for:

* Users who are completely new to TypeDB.
* Users who have used TypeDB a bit and want to solidify their understanding.
* Users who have used TypeDB a lot and want to recap the fundamentals.

You will not need any prior knowledge of TypeDB in order to complete this course.
